How to Play

Move your hand to control the green bar at the bottom of the screen. Try to catch the falling white circles to score points. Each missed circle costs you a life. The game gets faster as your score increases!

Tips: Use your index finger to precisely control the catcher. Make sure you have good lighting for better hand tracking.
